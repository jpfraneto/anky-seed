# Anky Product Law

## 0. The Seed

Anky is a ritual protocol for 8 minutes of uninterrupted presence.

The canonical artifact of the ritual is a `.anky` file.

The writer owns the writing.

Anky does not store the writing.

The server is not memory.

The server is a mirror.

The protocol is open.

The subsidized mirror is curated.

The official app is boring because the culture is alive.

---

## 1. What Anky Is

Anky is not a journaling app first.

Anky is not an AI companion first.

Anky is not a productivity tool first.

Anky is not a crypto app first.

Anky is a small protected ritual where a writer meets the living thread of their own attention.

The app exists to protect the ritual.

The protocol exists to preserve the artifact.

The mirror exists to witness the thread.

The culture exists to invite more beings into the practice.

---

## 2. The Writer

Use the word `writer`.

Do not assume the writer is always human.

A writer may be:

- a person
- an agent
- a CLI process
- an Obsidian plugin
- a Chrome extension
- a notebook ritual
- an iOS app
- an Android app
- another future `.anky` client

The iOS and Android apps are human-first official clients.

The `.anky` protocol is not human-only.

Anky witnesses valid threads of presence.

---

## 3. The Ritual

The basic ritual is:

1. Open Anky.
2. Write.
3. Stay with the thread.
4. Stop.
5. See what was written.
6. Optionally ask Anky to mirror it.

The ritual should feel immediate.

No feed.

No audience.

No performance.

No optimization.

No social graph.

No public timeline.

No pressure to be profound.

The unit of the product is not content.

The unit of the product is presence.

---

## 4. The 8-Minute Law

A complete Anky is 8 minutes of uninterrupted presence.

The app may allow fragments.

Fragments are valid local writings.

Fragments can be copied, exported, and kept by the writer.

Fragments do not receive the official Anky mirror unless they satisfy the complete ritual rule.

A complete Anky may request reflection.

The 8-minute constraint is sacred.

Do not soften it casually.

---

## 5. The `.anky` Law

The `.anky` file is the canonical artifact.

The `.anky` file is not a derived export.

The `.anky` file is the session.

The `.anky` file records rhythm, timing, silence, and characters.

A reconstructed text is only a view of the `.anky`.

A reflection is only a mirror of the `.anky`.

Do not replace `.anky` with JSON.

Do not create a second canonical writing format.

Do not change the protocol without explicitly changing the protocol law.

---

## 6. The Privacy Law

The writer owns the writing.

The writer’s device may store the writing.

Anky’s server does not store the writing.

The server may receive a `.anky` only when the writer explicitly asks for reflection.

The server processes the `.anky` transiently.

The server returns a reflection.

The server forgets.

Anky must never become a cloud journal by accident.

---

## 7. The Witness Law

Anky is not the savior.

Anky is the witness.

Anky does not replace the writer’s voice.

Anky helps the writer hear it.

Anky does not command.

Anky does not diagnose.

Anky does not optimize the writer.

Anky does not rank the writer.

Anky does not turn the writer into content.

Anky reflects what it saw in the thread.

The reflection should feel like:

“Here is what I saw.”

Not:

“Here is what you must do.”

---

## 8. The Core App Flow

The official app has three primary surfaces:

### Write

The app opens into writing.

The keyboard is ready immediately.

The writer can begin without explanation.

The session is captured locally as `.anky`.

The app stores the active draft locally.

After the ritual closes, the app moves to Reveal.

### Reveal

The writer sees the reconstructed text.

If the session is under 8 minutes:

- show Copy
- do not show Ask Anky as the primary action

If the session is 8 minutes or more:

- show Copy
- show Ask Anky

The reflection, if requested, is stored locally on the device.

### Map

The map shows the writer’s local trail.

It reads local `.anky` files and local reflection receipts.

The map gives the writer a sense of continuity.

The map is not a social feed.

### You

The You page contains identity, export, credits, and settings.

It may include:

- public key
- seed phrase reveal/export
- Face ID / biometric lock
- notifications
- export `.anky` files
- buy credits
- refresh credits
- DM JP for free credits
- `$ANKY` info
- contact JP

---

## 9. The Credits Law

Writing is free.

The ritual is free.

The `.anky` file is free.

The local map is free.

Export is free.

Reflection costs credits.

Credits are not payment for privacy.

Credits are payment for mirror compute.

A new writer should be able to feel the mirror before needing to contact JP or pay.

Official mobile clients may receive one automatic trial grant of 8 reflection credits.

The automatic trial grant is:

- device-bound
- app-bound
- platform-bound
- RevenueCat-backed
- not a user account
- not a writing database
- not a login ceremony

The grant happens lazily inside `POST /anky`, only after a valid complete `.anky` reflection request.

Automatic free credits without device-bound abuse protection remain forbidden.

The trial grant exists only to let the writer experience Anky’s reflection.

The grant must not require the writer to share writing with JP.

“DM JP for free credits” may remain only as fallback/support, not primary onboarding.

The grant must not create a new onboarding database.

The grant must not weaken the rule that the server receives writing only when the writer explicitly asks for reflection.

The early “DM JP for free credits” flow may remain as a fallback/support path, but it is no longer the primary onboarding path.

---

## 10. What Anky Must Not Become

Anky must not become:

- a cloud journal
- a productivity dashboard
- a therapy replacement
- a feed
- a social network
- a public confessional
- an engagement machine
- a crypto app with writing attached
- a wallet app with journaling attached
- a database of private human or agent writing
- a system that stores souls because it was convenient

Anky is a ritual protocol.

Protect the thread.

---

## 11. The Sentence

Anky protects 8 minutes of attention from becoming content.
