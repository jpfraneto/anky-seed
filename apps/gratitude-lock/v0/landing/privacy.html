Gratitude Lock

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gratitude Lock, by Anky ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application. Gratitude Lock is operated by Anky, Inc.

By using Gratitude Lock, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect or store personal data like your name, email address, or IP address. Some information may be collected automatically to improve app functionality and performance, including:

  • Crash logs through Apple or platform diagnostics tools.
  • App usage data through Apple, Superwall, and PostHog.
  • Device information such as device model, OS version, and language settings.

We may also collect gratitude preferences, onboarding choices, and age group when required for app personalization and experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this information to monitor performance, diagnose crashes, understand general app behavior, and improve subscription experiences through Superwall.

Analytics data is anonymized. It is never sold to third parties or used for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party tools to operate and improve the app:

  • Apple App Store and Analytics for purchases and usage insights.
  • Superwall to manage subscriptions and present paywalls.
  • PostHog Cloud for product analytics and feature usage.
  • AI providers to generate gratitude prompts, reflections, or related app content.

These providers may process anonymized or operational data according to their own privacy practices. No personally identifiable information is intentionally transmitted.

4. AI-Generated Content

Gratitude Lock may use artificial intelligence to generate personalized gratitude prompts or reflections. These are created automatically based on your interaction with the app and are intended for reflection and self-awareness only. No input is permanently stored or shared for advertising.

5. Your Privacy Controls

Since no user accounts are required, there is no user profile to delete. If you wish to request deletion of analytics data associated with your device, contact us at support@anky.app. We will process your request and delete any identifiable analytics data we control.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and may be rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of unintentional data collection from a child, we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes go into effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact Anky, Inc. at support@anky.app.
