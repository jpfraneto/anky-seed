# Anky Steiner Image Prompts

Use these prompts with the current Anky character reference attached. The images should feel like quiet ritual objects inside the app, not mascots competing for attention.

## Shared Art Direction

Anky is a small, warm, hand-painted threshold keeper. Preserve the existing silhouette and face, but render the character as a soft anthroposophic watercolor figure: lazure washes, Goethean color transitions, translucent mineral pigments, parchment grain, gentle asymmetry, visible hand texture, no glossy 3D, no flat vector UI, no cartoon comedy. The mood is intimate, attentive, and slightly numinous.

Palette: parchment cream, madder rose, muted violet, warm gold, pale apricot, soft umber, with thin ink accents. Keep backgrounds light enough to sit on Anky's dawn/paper surfaces.

Negative prompt: hard black outlines, plastic toy finish, Pixar or Disney character style, anime, photorealism, corporate mascot, UI mockup text, cluttered backgrounds, heavy contrast, neon colors, symmetrical logo mark, scary occult symbols, religious iconography, fake app screenshots.

Recommended output: transparent PNG where possible for character-only assets; otherwise soft parchment/lazure background. Generate at 2048 px on the long edge.

## 1. Onboarding: "This is Anky."

Suggested asset name: `anky-onboarding-closeness`

Aspect ratio: 4:5 portrait, transparent PNG or very pale parchment wash.

Prompt:

Create a hand-painted anthroposophic watercolor image of Anky, the small threshold keeper from the attached character reference, leaning slightly toward the viewer with open, attentive eyes and relaxed hands near the heart. The pose should portray closeness, trust, and emotional permission, as if Anky is quietly inviting the user to feel before they explain anything. Use Rudolf Steiner-inspired lazure watercolor washes, Goethean color transitions, parchment grain, warm gold and madder rose glows, soft violet shadows, and tender asymmetry. Keep the background minimal and breathable, with a faint circular warmth behind Anky but no halo, no symbols, and no text.

## 2. Onboarding: "What should I call you?"

Suggested asset name: `anky-name-badge`

Aspect ratio: 1:1, transparent PNG.

Prompt:

Create a hand-painted anthroposophic watercolor image of Anky from the attached character reference standing close to the viewer and wearing a simple red-and-white name badge sticker on the chest. The badge reads exactly: "Hi, my name is: Anky". The feeling is shy, personal, and gently funny, like Anky is introducing itself before asking the user's name. Use parchment texture, soft lazure pigment, muted red, warm cream, violet ink shadows, and imperfect hand-lettering that remains readable. Avoid movie-character style, glossy sticker effects, or exaggerated comedy.

## 3. Onboarding: "Choose a daily writing target."

Suggested asset name: `anky-daily-target`

Aspect ratio: 4:5 portrait, transparent PNG or pale parchment wash.

Prompt:

Create a hand-painted anthroposophic watercolor image of Anky from the attached character reference sitting beside a small golden thread stretched between minute marks from 1 to 8. Anky is not commanding the user; it is gently measuring a floor of practice, holding one end of the thread with care. The image should communicate "a daily target that supports you, never traps you." Use Rudolf Steiner-inspired lazure washes, warm gold, parchment cream, quiet violet, madder rose, hand-painted numerals or tiny tick marks, and a calm ritual mood. Keep the composition simple enough to sit above a slider in onboarding.

## 4. Onboarding: "When your apps knock, I'll answer."

Suggested asset name: `anky-notification-knock`

Aspect ratio: 4:5 portrait, transparent PNG or pale dawn wash.

Prompt:

Create a hand-painted anthroposophic watercolor image of Anky from the attached character reference standing inside a warm doorway, listening to a soft knock from outside. Use lazure watercolor, parchment grain, gold door light, violet exterior shadows, madder rose warmth, and subtle hand-drawn texture. No phone mockups, no notification banners, no clutter.

## 5. Gate Setup: "Put a door before the noise."

Suggested asset name: `anky-door-before-noise`

Aspect ratio: 4:5 portrait, transparent PNG or pale parchment wash.

Prompt:

Create a hand-painted anthroposophic watercolor image of Anky from the attached character reference placing a small wooden-gold door between the viewer and a soft cloud of noisy app shapes. The app shapes should be abstract rounded squares with muted colors, not recognizable logos. Anky's posture is calm and grounded, holding the door in place with both hands as a boundary, not a punishment. The image should communicate "choose what waits behind the door." Use Rudolf Steiner-inspired lazure washes, warm parchment, violet haze for noise, gold threshold light, madder rose accents, and tactile handmade pigment texture. Avoid locks, chains, prison imagery, or aggressive blocking symbolism.

## 6. Writing Interface: Subtle Anky Eyes

Suggested asset name: `anky-writing-eyes`

Aspect ratio: 3:1 wide, transparent PNG.

Prompt:

Create a very subtle transparent PNG of only Anky's eyes and a hint of brow from the attached character reference, rendered as soft anthroposophic watercolor pigment. The eyes should feel like a quiet witness at the top of the writing surface, replacing the current sun glyph. Make it extremely low visual weight: pale umber ink, warm gold flecks, faint violet wash, soft edges, no full face, no mouth, no body, no hard outline. It should be readable at low opacity and small size, like a gentle presence in parchment rather than a mascot.

## 7. Settings: "Customize your Anky experience"

Suggested asset name: `anky-settings-screwdriver`

Aspect ratio: 1:1, transparent PNG.

Prompt:

Create a hand-painted anthroposophic watercolor image of Anky from the attached character reference in a top-right-friendly pose, holding a small screwdriver and adjusting a tiny golden mechanism made of threads, sliders, and a little gear. The image should resemble settings without feeling mechanical or technical: Anky is tuning the ritual gently. Use Rudolf Steiner-inspired lazure watercolor, parchment cream, warm gold, muted violet shadows, soft madder rose, hand-painted texture, and a clear silhouette that can sit in the upper right of the Settings screen. Keep the screwdriver visible but delicate. No hard tech UI, no wrench crossed with gear logo, no clutter.
